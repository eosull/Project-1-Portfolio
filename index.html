Eoin O'Sullivan is an artist based in Limerick, Ireland. Initially working in music and sound design, in recent times he has moved towards visual work exploring photography, collage and drawing. During his studies he investigated creative uses for technology with a particular interest in capturing and reimagining sound. He has produced albums and radio documentaries that respond to communities, sociatal issues and creative practices.

Since the release of 'Boundaries' (2021), which can be heard on Bandcamp he has been working mainly with analogue photography, mixed-media collage and drawing. This website presents some of this recent work. Feel free to send a message via the contact page regarding commisions, collaborations or just to chat about some of the work.
